This project focused on understanding how data is transmitted across different media, starting from theoretical simulation to the physical deployment and optimization of a wireless transmission system.

The objective was to master the entire lifecycle of a transmission setup: planning the topology, establishing reliable physical links, and fine-tuning wireless performance to ensure optimal signal quality.

## Technical Implementation

### Network Simulation & Planning

Before any physical intervention, the project began with a comprehensive simulation phase. This allowed for a precise visualization of the data flow and helped in identifying potential bottlenecks or interference zones.

- **Topology Modeling**: Designing a virtual representation of the network to optimize the placement of critical components.
- **Predictive Analysis**: Using simulation tools to estimate signal range and throughput before deploying hardware.
- **Problem Identification**: Early detection of configuration issues, ensuring a smoother transition to the physical deployment phase.

### Physical Infrastructure & Interconnection

The second phase involved the hands-on deployment of the network backbone. I was responsible for establishing reliable connections between two strategic points (A and B), which required a deep understanding of physical transmission media.

- **Hardware Setup**: Configuring and interconnecting professional-grade routers, switches, and cabling.
- **Link Integrity**: Ensuring data integrity by adhering to strict wiring standards and device configuration protocols.
- **Latency Optimization**: Fine-tuning hardware settings to minimize transmission delays and maximize reliability across the point-to-point link.

### WiFi Optimization & Signal Analysis

The final and most critical phase was the deployment of a wireless transmission system. Strategic positioning of the WiFi antenna was paramount to achieving the best possible coverage and performance.

- **Antenna Alignment**: Applying signal propagation principles to find the optimal physical placement for the antenna.
- **Professional Diagnostics**: Utilizing **Acrylic WiFi**, a powerful network analysis tool, to conduct detailed signal measurements and interference analysis.
- **Environmental Calibration**: Adjusting the transmission parameters based on real-world test results to overcome physical obstacles and electromagnetic interference.
